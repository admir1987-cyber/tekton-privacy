Tekton — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tekton application ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following data only to provide its core functionality (creating construction daily logs):

  • Audio recordings — voice notes you record describing construction work. Recorded only when you start a recording. Audio is processed for AI analysis and then automatically deleted from your device; it is not stored on our servers.
  • Photographs — images you take or select to document the construction site. Photos are stored locally on your device and in your device gallery; they are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Location data — your approximate and precise location, used to attach weather conditions and the site location to your daily log.
  • Account email — your email address, used to create your account and sign in.
  • Text you enter — project details, names, and notes you type into the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Audio recordings and photographs are sent to our AI processing service to generate professional text summaries for your construction log. After the audio is analyzed, it is deleted from your device.
  • Photographs are saved locally on your device and to your device photo gallery so you can view, keep, and share them.
  • Location is used to fetch local weather data and to label the site location on the log.
  • Your account email is used only for authentication (sign-up and sign-in).
  • Your projects and daily logs (text) are stored securely in the cloud so you can access them across sessions and devices.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Supabase — secure cloud storage and database for your account and your text data (projects and daily logs). Photos and audio are NOT stored on Supabase.
  • Google Gemini (AI) — processes your audio and photos to generate text summaries. This processing is momentary and Google does not retain this data for these requests.
  • Open-Meteo — provides weather data based on your location.

These services process data only as needed to provide the App's features.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Your projects and daily logs (text) are stored in the cloud and tied to your account. Each user can only access their own data.
  • Photographs are stored only on your device (app folder and device gallery). They remain on your device until you delete them; deleting a project does not delete photos already saved to your gallery.
  • Audio recordings are temporary and are deleted from your device automatically after AI analysis.
  • You can delete individual projects and daily logs at any time within the App. Deleting a project also deletes its daily logs from our storage.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with advertisers. Data is shared only with the third-party services listed above, solely to operate the App.

6. Permissions

The App requests these device permissions:

  • Microphone — to record voice notes.
  • Camera — to take site photographs.
  • Photos / Storage — to save photographs to your device gallery.
  • Location — to attach weather and site location to logs.
  • Internet — to sync your text data and run AI analysis.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings, though some features may stop working.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for professional construction use and is not directed at children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below. You can also delete your account data directly within the App.

Tekton — Politika zasebnosti

Zadnja posodobitev: 30. junij 2026

Ta politika zasebnosti opisuje, kako aplikacija Tekton ("aplikacija", "mi") zbira, uporablja in varuje vaše podatke. Z uporabo aplikacije se strinjate z opisanimi praksami.

1. Podatki, ki jih zbiramo

Aplikacija zbira naslednje podatke izključno za delovanje svoje osnovne funkcije (ustvarjanje gradbenih dnevnikov):

  • Zvočni posnetki — glasovne beležke, ki jih posnamete pri opisu gradbenih del. Posnamejo se le, ko sami začnete snemanje. Zvok se obdela za analizo z umetno inteligenco in nato samodejno izbriše iz vaše naprave; ne shranjuje se na naših strežnikih.
  • Fotografije — slike, ki jih posnamete ali izberete za dokumentiranje gradbišča. Fotografije se shranijo lokalno v vaši napravi in v galeriji naprave; ne nalagajo se na naše strežnike.
  • Podatki o lokaciji — vaša približna in natančna lokacija, uporabljena za pripis vremenskih razmer in lokacije gradbišča v dnevnik.
  • E-pošta računa — vaš e-poštni naslov, uporabljen za ustvarjanje računa in prijavo.
  • Besedilo, ki ga vnesete — podatki o projektu, imena in opombe, ki jih vnesete.

2. Kako uporabljamo vaše podatke

  • Zvočni posnetki in fotografije se pošljejo naši storitvi za obdelavo z umetno inteligenco, ki ustvari strokovne besedilne povzetke za vaš dnevnik. Po analizi se zvok izbriše iz vaše naprave.
  • Fotografije se shranijo lokalno v napravi in v galeriji naprave, da jih lahko pregledujete, hranite in delite.
  • Lokacija se uporablja za pridobitev lokalnih vremenskih podatkov in oznako lokacije gradbišča.
  • E-pošta računa se uporablja izključno za prijavo (registracija in vpis).
  • Vaši projekti in dnevniki (besedilo) se varno shranijo v oblaku, da do njih dostopate med sejami in napravami.

3. Storitve tretjih oseb

Aplikacija za delovanje uporablja naslednje storitve tretjih oseb:

  • Supabase — varno shranjevanje v oblaku in baza podatkov za vaš račun in besedilne podatke (projekti in dnevniki). Fotografije in zvok se NE shranjujejo na Supabase.
  • Google Gemini (UI) — obdela vaše zvočne posnetke in fotografije za ustvarjanje besedilnih povzetkov. Obdelava je trenutna in Google teh podatkov za te zahteve ne hrani.
  • Open-Meteo — zagotavlja vremenske podatke glede na vašo lokacijo.

4. Shranjevanje in hramba podatkov

  • Vaši projekti in dnevniki (besedilo) so shranjeni v oblaku in povezani z vašim računom. Vsak uporabnik lahko dostopa le do svojih podatkov.
  • Fotografije so shranjene le v vaši napravi (mapa aplikacije in galerija naprave). Ostanejo v napravi, dokler jih ne izbrišete; izbris projekta ne izbriše fotografij, ki so že shranjene v galeriji.
  • Zvočni posnetki so začasni in se samodejno izbrišejo iz naprave po analizi.
  • Posamezne projekte in dnevnike lahko kadar koli izbrišete v aplikaciji. Izbris projekta izbriše tudi njegove dnevnike iz našega shranjevanja.

5. Deljenje podatkov

Vaših osebnih podatkov ne prodajamo. Podatkov ne delimo z oglaševalci. Podatki se delijo le z zgoraj navedenimi storitvami tretjih oseb, izključno za delovanje aplikacije.

6. Dovoljenja

Aplikacija zahteva naslednja dovoljenja naprave:

  • Mikrofon — za snemanje glasovnih beležk.
  • Kamera — za fotografiranje gradbišča.
  • Fotografije / Shramba — za shranjevanje fotografij v galerijo naprave.
  • Lokacija — za pripis vremena in lokacije gradbišča.
  • Internet — za sinhronizacijo besedilnih podatkov in analizo z UI.

Dovoljenja lahko kadar koli prekličete v nastavitvah naprave, vendar nekatere funkcije morda ne bodo več delovale.

7. Zasebnost otrok

Aplikacija je namenjena strokovni gradbeni uporabi in ni namenjena otrokom, mlajšim od 13 let.

8. Vaše pravice

Dostop do svojih podatkov, popravek ali izbris lahko zahtevate prek spodnjega e-naslova. Podatke računa lahko izbrišete tudi neposredno v aplikaciji.
